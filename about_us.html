Our Story

Born in 2022, fashion brand Nedd Fashion has determined its mission to help Vietnamese fashion develop more and more alongside the world. Today, young people are standing by the great opportunities of modern society, the development of information technology, the revolution of social networking sites to assert themselves. Besides, Nedd Fashion understands that young people are also facing pressures and challenges that urge themselves to show themselves compared to others.
